Hover over a node to:

  • show title of the corresponding post, or name of corresponding approach, on the graph
  • read a preview of the post, or description of the approach, in this space
  • hovered node has yellow border

Click a node to:

  • show the post, or description of the approach, in the space to the right
  • show author's bio, in this space (click author's name in post to restore if needed)
  • zoom graph to that node and others linked to it
    • clicking approach node shows posts that take that approach
    • clicking post node shows approaches that post takes
    • other nodes and edges become faded
    • clicking background zooms back out
  • clicked nodes have green borders.

Hover over edges to show source of association between post and approach--author's discipline, or ideas in post from outside author's discipline
