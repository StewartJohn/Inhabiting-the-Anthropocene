Hover over a node to:

  • show title of the corresponding post, on the graph
  • read a preview of the post, in this space
  • hovered node has yellow border

Click a node to:

  • show the post, in the space to the right
  • show author's bio, in this space (click author's name in post to restore if needed)
  • zoom graph to that node and others linked to it
    • other nodes and edges become faded
    • clicking background zooms back out
  • clicked nodes have green borders.

Hover over an edge to show:

  • comment on relationship between linked posts, provided by the author of one of them, in this space
  • description of posts as similar, different, or by same author, on the graph
  • arrow on link indicates author's suggested reading order
