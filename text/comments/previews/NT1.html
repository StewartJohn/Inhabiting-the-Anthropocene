Reading post on Atleo, "Principles of Tsawalk: An Indigenous Approach to Global Crisis"

Noah Theriault

Geographer Jamie Lormier has argued that the "recent diagnosis of the Anthropocene represents the public death of the modern understanding of Nature removed from society." In the Anthropocene, there is no outside to society and nothing that is purely natural. Accepting this reality, one hopes, will force accountability for our collective impacts.
