Reading post on Steele and Mittal, "Building 'Equitable' Urban Resilience: The Challenge for Cities"

Meghan Wieters

In their chapter, Steele and Mittal discuss the key concepts of resilience and equity in the urban environment. The urban planning discipline centers on this challenge: how do we as professionals provide for the greater public good, health, safety and general welfare?
