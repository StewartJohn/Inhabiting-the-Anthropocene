"Memes as a Dimension of the Human Niche"

Ingo Schlupp

Though human beings can be understood biologically as one species among others, we have one particularly prominent, even defining, feature: culture. Already our close relatives, chimpanzees, show simpler forms of culture, but we know that our hominoid ancestors evolved the capacity to develop massively complex culture at some time on the evolutionary path that led to modern humans.
