This graph represents the conceptual structure of the set of posts in terms of four disciplinary approaches.
Squares represent approaches:
Descriptive approach--characteristic of natural sciences
Interpretative approach--characteristic of social sciences
Normative approach--characteristic of humanities
Practical approach--characteristic of applied disciplines
Circles represent posts:
  • Color is based on author's discipline--see the Explanation of Approaches page
  • Initials indicate author:
    • 1 = reading post, 2 = reflection post
    • see List of Posts for details
Edges link posts to approaches they take:
  • Solid line indicates approach based on author's home discipline
  • Broken line indicates an additional approach found in the post
