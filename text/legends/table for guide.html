the Approaches and Themes vie

You choose which graph to activate with the . When you select a view a more detailed explanation of it appears in this window, and a brief explanation appears below the graph, to the left.

  • Approaches View. This shows nodes for the posts and for the four approaches taken the authors, and edges showing which posts take which approach. This table explains how to operate this view:
        Post Node Approach
      Node      		 Edge
      Hover Shows title; author bio in Comments Shows title; brief explanation in Comments Shows title; brief explanation in Comments
      Click Post shows in this window; bio remains until mouse hovers elsewhere in graph Long explanation shows in this window; bio remains until mouse hovers elsewhere in graph  
  • Hovering the mouse over a post node shows its title and the author's photo and bio in the Comments area below the graph to the right. Clicking brings up the post in this window, and makes the bio stay until the mouse hovers elsewhere in the graph. This functionality is the same in all four views.
  • Hovering above an approach node shows an explanation the Comments area; clicking will keep it there until the mouse hovers elsewhere in the graph.
  • Hovering over edges shows a brief explanation of why the linked post is associated with that approach (either due to the author's home discipline, or because it appears in the post).
  • By default the Comments section explains the color scheme for the post nodes.

Themes View. This shows nodes for the posts and also for the four themes explored in the posts. Hovering the mouse above the theme nodes shows an explanation in the Comments area below the graph to the right.
