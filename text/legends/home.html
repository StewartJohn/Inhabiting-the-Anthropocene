This website produces graphs that show the relationships among a series of blog posts (circles), based on the themes (polgons) they touch on and the approaches (squares) their authors take.

Use "Select a View" (right end of the the bar below the graph; click horizontal line button if not visible) to see how the nodes can be linked together in different ways. From those views you can click on nodes to read the associated posts.

IF YOU DO NOT SEE THE NODES IT IS LIKELY AN ISSUE WITH YOUR BROWSER. THE SITE IS BEST VIEWED IN FIREFOX OR CHROME; IT WORKS IN SAFARI 8, BUT NOT 7 AND BELOW OR 9.
