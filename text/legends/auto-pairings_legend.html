This graph represents linkages among posts that emerged from a simple social network analysis that calculated the number of approaches, and outlooks on themes, each pair of posts had in common.

Circles represent posts:
  • Color is based on author's discipline--see the Explanation of Approaches page
  • Initials indicate author:
    • 1 = reading post, 2 = reflection post
    • see List of Posts for details
Edges link posts which share one or more approach or outlook on a theme. The width of the line corresponds to the number of commonalities, so posts linked by wider lines are more similar than posts linked by thinner lines.
