Agency. The Anthropocene frames human activity as the decisive factor shaping Earth’s habitability—for humans and all other forms of life. Is this attribution of overwhelming influence to humanity correct? Or does it overstate human power and understate the workings of non-human factors in shaping the environment?

  • Broken line: post emphasizes human habitation as entangled with other factors
