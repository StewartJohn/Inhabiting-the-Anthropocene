Explanation of elements in Author-Supplied Pairings View

This graph presents suggestions the authors made about ways their posts can be paired with others.
  • For each of their own posts authors suggested one that was broadly similar and one that was broadly different.
  • The graph also links each author's own two posts.
  • Links can be toggled on and off using buttons to the right of the graph.

  • Color is based on author's discipline--see Approaches View [and Key (below graph).
