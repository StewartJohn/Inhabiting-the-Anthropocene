This graph represents suggestions the authors made about ways their posts can be paired with others--as broadly similar or broadly different.

Circles represent posts:
  • Color is based on author's discipline--see the Explanation of Approaches page
  • Initials indicate author:
    • 1 = reading post, 2 = reflection post
    • see List of Posts for details
Edges link posts identified by authors as similar or different (or posts by the same author):
  • Arrow indicates any suggestion of which post to read first
  • Color of line indicates author who suggested it
  • Style of line indicates suggested relation between post
    • Solid line = posts by same author (in that author's color)
    • Broken line = author suggesting link sees the posts as similar
    • Faint line = author suggesting link sees the posts as different
    • Hover on an edge to see explanation (in Comments below graph to right)
