List of Posts

  • These are the posts collected on this site, listed alphabetically by author.
  • Each author is assigned a color, based on his or her home discipline (see the Explanation of Approaches page).
  • Each author has two posts:
    • the first discusses a reading, identified in the post title
    • the second is a "reflection" on issues involved in habitation in the Anthropocene.
  • To read a particular post click the respective node in the graph.
  • To explore the relationships among the posts use the "Select a View" drop-down menu (right end of the the bar below the graph; click button if not visible) to call up a graph that represents the various approaches taken and themes explored in the set. You can read posts from these graphs by clicking on their respective nodes.
